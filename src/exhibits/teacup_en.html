Ivory-White Ground with Polychrome Floral and Bird Pattern Teacup and Saucer Set

Tea set from the Kangxi period of the Qing Dynasty


Teacup: height 4.6 cm, diameter 8.0 cm, base diameter 3.8 cm;
Saucer: height 1.8 cm, diameter 13.0 cm, base diameter 6.9 cm.

The teacup features a diamond-shaped rim, a deep belly, and a short ring foot. The saucer has an open mouth, diamond-flower shape, a shallow arc belly, and a short ring foot.

Both the teacup and the saucer features: The inner rims decorated with underglaze blue patterns on a brocade background. With the exterior first coated with an ivory-white glaze, over which polychrome floral patterns are painted. The interiors adorned with polychrome floral motifs, and the bottom bears an underglaze blue mark.
